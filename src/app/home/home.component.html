Our Philosopy

ShareCab is one of the innovative ride-sharing companies. ShareCab integrates city transportation for customers and driver-partners onto a mobile technology platform ensuring convenient, transparent, and quick service fulfilment. ShareCab is focused on leveraging the best of technology and building innovative solutions ground-up, that are relevant at global scale.

ShareCab Play the first Shared Cab platform for ride-sharing is expected to be launched by 2019 January, transforming commuting experiences and setting the tone for global innovation in this space. Beyond offering a highly personal experience for users during their rides, ShareCab Play also allows loyalty customers the high quality interactive and productive experience. Using the ShareCab mobile app, passengers can connect with over 100+ driver-partners across cabs, auto-rickshaws, and vans. Driven by a hyperlocal approach, ShareCab is committed to its mission of building mobility for a billion people.
